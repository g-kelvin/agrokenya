These Are Our Services

We Value Our Customers

Food Safety

The domestic market system is highly fragmented and poorly governed leading to high food loss and lack of private or public system for food safety assurance. Over 90% of consumers in Kenya do not understand food safety and intrinsic food quality standards. Food Safety has become an integral part of food security globally. Coming from background of export horticulture, coffee and tea industry, the founders of AKCS are very passionate about food safety. We have the experience and expertise to develop and manage food safety management systems throughout the value chain.
Using HACCP Principles and risk-based assessment, we deliver sustainable food safety management systems based on internationally recognized standards, e.g. BRC, FSSC 22,000 and ISO 22,000. Our food safety experts have several years of experience developing and managing food safety systems across the value chain from exporter to retailer level.

Extension Services

Over 80% of farmers in developing countries have limited knowledge on production and lack access to extension services. Extension service provision has declined in the last two decades in Kenya and other parts of Africa. The rising farmers’ population is desperate for professional agronomic and extension services.
AKCS has a team of experienced crop-specific agronomists who provide tailored solutions to your agronomy needs. Our Agronomists are independent and as such will only recommend products based on sound professional judgment.

Audits & Audits Preparation

Preparation for audits can be a challenge and time-consuming. At AKCS, we work to guide you towards compliance.
In addition, we also carry out a pre-audit against all the major GAP and Food Safety standards to help benchmark your organization’s level of preparedness for the main audit.

Market Linkagess

The missing link in the value chain for small and medium producers is the market; a phenomenon farmer have to grapple with as its complex and surrounded with so many challenges. We at AKCS we offer market linkages or advise for products grown using traceable good agricultural practices. We have a pool of buyers who are ready to buy as long as they are assured the products are safe.
