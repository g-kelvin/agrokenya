These Are Our Services

We Value Our Customers

Management Consultancy

Agro Kenya Services (AKS) understands the importance of agriculture as the backbone of most of the world economies. Agriculture is becoming even more important into the future due to growing demand rising from increasing global population. Many countries are encouraging their citizens to undertake agribusiness as a way of taming unemployment.
At AKCS, we provide professional advice on the ventures to undertake for small, medium and large-scale producers. We offer overall farm management. We have a team of experienced Agronomists and Farm managers who will manage the investment on your behalf. We also offer field advisory where we send our agronomy consultants to visit a farmer on agreed schedule with the owner and provide a technical report on areas of improvement.

GAP

Good Agricultural Practice is fundamental element of sustainability in the agricultural value chain. Resources are limiting and have to be used efficiently so as to protect the environment and the consumers. As a result, it requires a level of expertise to make rational decision on what and how to use the limiting resources.
At AKCS, we have a team of experts Agronomists and Integrated Crop management (ICM) experts who will walk with you and advise you on the best approach to take right from cultivation to harvesting. We cover various areas on soil and fertility management, fertilizer recommendations, pest and diseases management, pre and post harvest handling techniques and any other operation requiring our input. Our Agronomist will walk with you to deliver the best advice for optimum yield and cost-effective production.
